Pipeline
Given multimodal instructions, the Co-Reasoning Director reasons and plans hierarchical, fine-grained positive and negative storylines in a multi-turn dialogue manner, and the spatio-temporal cascade pipeline generates coherent, long-form, high-resolution avatar videos in parallel.
Responsible Use Statement
The images and audio shown in these demonstrations are either public-domain, pre-licensed, or generated by our models. They are presented solely to illustrate the technical capabilities of our research—specifically, the generation of coherent facial expressions and natural body movements from multimodal inputs. If you have any concerns about the content, please contact liujiwen@kuaishou.com and we will respond promptly.
